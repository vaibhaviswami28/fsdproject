Vitthal Humbe

Visual Storyteller & Developer

Artist Statement

"I believe art is the bridge between the logical structure of the world and the chaotic beauty of the human spirit. My work explores the space where reality ends and imagination begins—drawing heavy inspiration from the emotional depth of anime and the philosophical roots of Indian culture."

The Story

The Journey Begins: My artistic journey wasn't born in a studio, but in the margins of notebooks. Influenced by the epic narratives of Vinland Saga and One Piece, I realized early on that a single sketch could carry the weight of a thousand words.

The Discipline: Just as I write code to build systems, I sketch to build worlds. I maintain a daily practice—a commitment to putting pencil to paper every single day. This "daily save" mechanism allows me to capture fleeting thoughts and evolve them into structured visual concepts.

The Vision: Today, "Chitra Katha" serves as my sanctuary. It is where I experiment with character design, explore Hindu philosophical themes, and work towards my long-term goal of creating a full-fledged animated narrative that heals and inspires.
